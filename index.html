Meet the Characters

Mr Fish

If you asked him to narrow it down, Mr. Fish would probably say he has two main problems: everyone and everything. At least that’s how he appears..

Pip

If the entire eastern seaboard had a blackout, you could plug Pip in and you'd have wattage leftover. She's so high-energy she makes Red Bull look like NyQuil.

Shimmer

Shimmer is blessed with ethereal beauty and magical powers. She’s the prize that every fish in the ocean seeks. But the truth is, she isn’t magic at all. And the blessing feels more like a curse.
